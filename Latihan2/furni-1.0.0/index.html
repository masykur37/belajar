Honours

Very few clubs anywhere in the world have won so many titles. The club's greatest pride and joy remain the five European Cup titles won in Wembley (1992, 2011), Paris (2006), Rome (2009), Berlin (2015) the FIFA Club World Cup (2009, 2011 and 2015)

  • Champions League - 5 (1991-92,2005-06,2008-09,2010-11,2014-15)
  • FIFA Club World Cup - 3 (2009-10, 2011-12, 2015-16)
  • European Cup Winners' Cup - 4 (1978-79, 1981-82, 1988-89, 1996-97)
  • Copa del Rey - 31 (1909-10,1911-12,1912-13,1919-20,1921-22,1924-25)
  • European Super Cup - 5 (1992-93, 1997-98, 2009-10, 2011-12, 2015-16)
  • Spanish League Championship - 27 (1928-29,1944-45,1947-48,1948-49,1951-52,)
